Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LockBridge Connect collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when users access the Service.

Effective date: 1 January 2026
Last updated: 1 January 2026

1. Introduction

LockBridge Connect ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy.

LockBridge Connect is an independent online platform that helps users request locksmith-related assistance and connect with independent local service providers. We do not provide locksmith services directly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using our platform, including:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Location details, such as city or ZIP code
  • Description of your locksmith-related request

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device and operating system
  • Pages visited and interaction data
  • Referring URLs
  • Approximate geographic location

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analyze usage, and support marketing efforts. For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and route your request to relevant independent service providers
  • Enable providers to contact you directly
  • Operate and improve the platform
  • Communicate with you, including support and updates
  • Analyze usage and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and misuse

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Service Providers (Core Functionality)

When you submit a request, your information may be shared with independent locksmith service providers so they can contact you regarding your request.

These providers operate independently and are not employees or agents of LockBridge Connect.

4.2 With Technology and Analytics Partners

We may share limited data with third-party providers such as analytics services, advertising platforms, hosting providers, and infrastructure providers.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to comply with legal obligations, protect rights, safety, or property, or prevent fraud or abuse.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including secure data transmission, access controls, monitoring, and security practices. However, no system is completely secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access your personal data, request correction or deletion, object to or restrict processing, request data portability, or withdraw consent where applicable. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is identified, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

9. U.S. Privacy Rights (Including California)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what data is collected, request deletion, opt out of data sharing where applicable, and receive non-discriminatory treatment for exercising privacy rights. To exercise these rights, contact us below.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers where permitted by law.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for providing the Service, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and business operations. After that, data is deleted or anonymized.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact LockBridge Connect at support@lockbridgeconnect.com.
