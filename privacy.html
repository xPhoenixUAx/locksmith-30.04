Privacy Policy

This policy explains how LockBridge Connect may collect, use, and share information submitted through this locksmith referral platform.

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect information you submit through forms, including name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, service category, urgency, preferred contact method, and message details. We may also collect technical data such as browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, referring URLs, and cookie identifiers.

How We Use Information

We use information to process locksmith-related requests, contact users about requests, connect users with independent local providers or referral partners, operate and improve the website, prevent misuse, measure performance, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing With Providers and Partners

LockBridge Connect may share request information with independent service providers, referral partners, vendors, analytics providers, and support services. Providers are independent and their own privacy practices may apply once they contact you.

Cookies and Analytics

We may use essential, analytics, marketing, and third-party cookies to operate the site, understand usage, and improve request routing. See the Cookie Policy for more information.

User Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also opt out of certain communications by contacting us.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, but no website or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to support@lockbridgeconnect.com.

LockBridgeConnect

LockBridge Connect is an independent locksmith referral platform that helps users request locksmith-related help and connect with local service providers. LockBridge Connect does not directly perform locksmith services, and all providers are independent.
