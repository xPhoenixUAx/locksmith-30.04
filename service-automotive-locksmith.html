Before approving service

Providers may require proof of vehicle ownership or identification before service. This website does not provide vehicle opening, key programming, or immobilizer bypass instructions.

Service scope

Automotive key and vehicle access requests

Automotive requests may relate to a locked vehicle, a lost or damaged key, a key fob concern, a trunk access issue, or a question about replacement options. The exact provider conversation can depend on the vehicle make, model, year, key type, location, and proof of ownership requirements.

LockBridge Connect does not explain how to open vehicles, program keys, bypass immobilizers, or defeat vehicle security systems. The platform helps users submit a request so independent providers may discuss lawful service options, proof requirements, timing, and pricing when available.

Standards users should ask about

  • Identification and vehicle ownership proof before service.
  • Whether the provider is equipped for the vehicle make, model, year, and key type.
  • Written pricing for service call, key-related work, parts, and after-hours fees.
  • Any limitations, warranty terms, or dealership-related considerations.

Information to prepare

  • Vehicle make, model, year, and approximate location.
  • Whether the key is lost, broken, locked inside, or not recognized.
  • Photo ID, registration, title, insurance, or other ownership documents.
  • Preferred contact method and whether the vehicle is in a safe location.
