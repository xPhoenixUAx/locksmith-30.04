Before approving service

Fast should still feel clear.

Urgency can make vague pricing or unclear credentials feel normal. Use these checkpoints before approving work with any independent provider.

Ask Confirm Approve
Provider identityConfirm the provider name, business details, and who is contacting you.
Pricing firstAsk about trip fees, labor, parts, and payment terms before work begins.
Written termsRequest clear service terms, scope, and any warranty information when available.
Ownership proofExpect property, vehicle, or authorization checks before service is approved.

LockBridge Connect does not guarantee provider licensing, pricing, response time, or work quality. Users should verify all terms directly with the independent provider.
