Service explanation

Organize home lock and access requests clearly

LockBridge Connect routes residential requests toward independent local providers who may be available to discuss home access issues, lock updates, rekeying, and smart lock requests. The platform does not directly perform locksmith work or employ providers.

What providers may evaluate

Details that help the conversation

  • Door type, lock type, and the reason for the request.
  • Whether proof of residence or authorization is needed.
  • Pricing, service scope, parts, warranties, and timing.
  • Whether the request is urgent or can be scheduled.

Verification reminder

Users should verify provider credentials, identity, pricing, license requirements where applicable, and written service terms before approving residential work.
